About Us

From Early Pioneer to Leader in Multi-Industries Today

With rubber-related chemicals remain the heart of our business, Centre West has evolved into a leading supplier for various industries across the region. Our offerings include all kinds of chemicals for coating, ink, paint, ceramic, foundry and chemical dispersions, as well as industrial processing machinery.

Today, Centre West has expanded from its core strength in trading to manucfacturing. Through Alpha Nanotech, we pave the way for new innovations and developments in chemical dispersions for the rubber industry. It marks our leap forward to better materials, because that is what iit means to be a true centre of excellence.
