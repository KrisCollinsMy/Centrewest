Over Four Decades of Expertise Here to Serve You

At Centre West, high quality raw materials are we do best. Since our establishment in 1975, our core business has grown from the supply of rubber and latex-related chemicals to a multi-industry portfolio today.

Supported by in-depth knowledge, expertise and service excellence honed from over four decades of experience, it is why Centre West is the choice amongst top brands in various industries; a dynamic partner for ventures in Malaysia and Asia-Pacific; the ONLY centre you need.
