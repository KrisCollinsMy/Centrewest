MILESTONES

One Name, Five Dynamic Entities

Consisting of five subsidaries, the Centre West Group of Companies exist to fullfill differing needs of different industries. Each subsidary is a dynamic enity, specialising and catering to specific needs but to work together to uphold Centre West's values and reputation.

The companies are led by Centre West Resources Sdn. Bhd. - an investment holding arm responsible for driving the growth and expansion of the group.
