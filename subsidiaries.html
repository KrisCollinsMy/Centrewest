subsidiaries

One Name, Five Dynamic Entities

Consisting of five subsidiaries, the Centre West Group of Companies exist to fullfill differing needs of different industries. Each subsidary is a dynamic enity, specialising and catering to specific needs but to work together to uphold Centre West's values and reputation.

The companies are led by Centre West Resources Sdn. Bhd. - an investment holding arm responsible for driving the growth and expansion of the group.

Centre West Industrial Supplies Sdn. Bhd.

A One-Stop Shop for raw materials and cutting-edge machinery used in the rubber and latex industries.

Key Features

  • More than 4 decades of experience in the rubber
  • Comprehensive range of products
  • Reliable source of information on the latest chemicals, machinery & equipment
  • Obtained ISO9001: 2015 certification

Centre West Chemicals Sdn. Bhd.

Focuses on the supply and distribution of industrial chemicals to various industries:
  • Adhesives
  • Batik
  • Building Materials
  • Chemical Manufacturing
  • Coatings
  • Cementitious Products
  • Electroplating
  • Foundry
  • Paint
  • Plastic
  • Printing Ink
  • Tiles and Ceramics

Key Features

  • Extensive supply network in the ASEAN region
  • Acquired exclusive agency and distribution rights in the specialty chemicals from local and overseas manufacturers
  • Obtained ISO9001: 2015 certification

Alpha Nanotech Sdn. Bhd.

Frontiers new innovations for the rubber and polymer industries.

Key Features

  • Manufacturer of high quality water-based colloidal dispersions.
  • Strategic location close to Port Klang. Malaysia's main port - allowing for better supply lines.
  • R&D facility run by highly-trained and experienced personnel
  • Latest manufacturing facilities and state-of-the-art quality control instruments
  • Obtained ISO14001: 2015 and ISO9001: 2015 certifications

Centre West International (Thailand) Co., Ltd

An international expansion developed to tap into Thailand's markets for rubber, chemical and machinery supplies.

Key Features

  • An international source for raw materials and machinery providing customers with more cost-effective and alternative options.

Centre West Resources Sdn. Bhd.

An investment holding arm responsible for driving the growth and expansion of the group.

Key Features

  • Investment holding arm and management branch of Centre West
  • Provides resources for the Centre West Group and Companies
  • Perform comprehensive assessments on the Group's current growth, in order to bring them to greater heights
